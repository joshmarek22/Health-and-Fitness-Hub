High-Protein French Toast

High protein French toast is a nutritious and satisfying breakfast option that incorporates a significant amount of protein into the classic French toast recipe. By using protein-rich ingredients like eggs, egg whites, and protein powder, this variation of French toast provides a substantial boost in protein content compared to traditional recipes. The result is a delicious and filling meal that helps support muscle growth and repair, while still offering the familiar flavors and comfort of the beloved French toast dish.

Copycat Chipotle Burrito Bowl

A copycat Chipotle burrito bowl is a homemade version of the restaurant's popular dish. It features a base of rice or greens, protein (chicken, steak, tofu), and customizable toppings like beans, pico de gallo, cheese, guacamole, and sour cream. Enjoy Chipotle flavors at home, with the flexibility to adjust ingredients as you like.
